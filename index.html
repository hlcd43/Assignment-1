About us

Our phisolophy

At here, we believe that environments shape our thoughts and feelings, whether we are at home, at work or on the go. We work every day to better our surroundings through the power of design. A family business in its third generation, We follows an environmental, cultural and commercial mission.

Our Campus and our Design Museum with its exhibitions, design archives and a comprehensive furniture collection inspire visitors and employees alike. They further the understanding of design and architecture‘s role in defining the future we live in.

Environmental consciousness finds expression in every aspect of our work. It is manifested in how we develops and manufactures its products, in the sourcing of raw materials and the organisation of its supply chain. Every new insight is regarded as an opportunity for further development.

Work with us

Run as a family business for the past eighty years, We believes in lasting relationships with customers, employees and designers, and stands for durable products, sustainable growth and the power of good design.

We value commitment and flexibility. We are looking for passionate employees with agile minds and skilled hands to help us achieve our company goals.
